How to Use & Interpret

How to Use

  1. Core Goal (Center): Start by defining your main objective in the absolute center of the grid.
  2. Sub-Goals (Inner Ring): Identify 8 key areas or pillars needed to achieve your Core Goal. Fill these in the center cells of the surrounding 8 blocks.
  3. Action Items (Outer Rings): For each Sub-Goal, list 8 specific, actionable steps in the surrounding cells.

Interpretation

  • Visual Clarity: The grid allows you to see the "Big Picture" and "Specific Actions" simultaneously.
  • Balance: Ensure your Sub-Goals cover different aspects (e.g., Health, Career, Finance, Relationships) for a balanced approach.
  • Flow: Success flows from the outside in. Completing Action Items leads to achieving Sub-Goals, which ultimately realizes the Core Goal.
